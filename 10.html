Bangladesh, a riverine country, is filled with the boundless beauty of nature. Its rivers, lush green fields, and tranquil village environments bring peace to the hearts of people. The diverse expressions of tradition are scattered throughout the land. The simplicity and warmth of the people of Bengal set them apart from anywhere else in the world. Interwoven with this natural beauty are tales of history, culture, and struggle, which define the identity of Bengal.
