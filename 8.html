Tim Berners-Lee is a Britishs computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web. In 1989, he proposed the idea for the Web, and in 1991, he launched the first website.

Berners-Lee developed foundational technologies like HTML (HyperText Markup Language), HTTP and URL Uniform Resource Locator(HyperText Transfer Protocol), which made the internet accessible and functional.

He was born on June 8, 1955, in London. Today, he continues to work on issues related to open web standards.
In 2014, he founded the "Web Foundation" with the goal of keeping the internet open for all.
